Job Seekers Board

Job search is not a simple process for any person so this project is supposed to simulate a bulletin board for people who looking for new job in order to facilitate the process and make recruiters find their candidates.

Create Your Own Posts!

Users can create a job post on the board by entering their personal details in the form on the left side which contains a title, phone, email and text area where they can tell about themselves briefly and also leave contact details in order for recruiters or people who have job offers to be able to contact them. If you came here and you are looking for a job leave a note do not be shy!
